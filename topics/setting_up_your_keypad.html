Setting Up Your Keypad

You’ll use your Keypad to arm and disarm your Alarm, so we recommend that you place it in the hallway or near the door you most often use to exit and enter. Some people install a second Keypad in their bedroom for easy access at night.

  1. Connect:Using the steps shown in the app, plug the Keypad into a socket with the included USB cable and wall adapter.
  2. Configure: Give your Keypad a unique name and add it to a room. Don’t forget, you must create a personal access code, which is used to arm and disarm your Alarm.
  3. Install:The Keypad can be mounted flush on a wall or placed on a table using the reversible bracket, and can be left plugged in or unplugged. Follow the instructions on the next page.
