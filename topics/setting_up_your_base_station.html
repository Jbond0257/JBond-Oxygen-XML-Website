Setting Up Your Base Station

Your Ring Base Station connects all of your other devices to the ring app, so you can access your alarm system wherever you are.

Note: The mobile app will guide you through the process. Follow the icons onscreen to ensure you have correctly connected the Base Station.

To allow the app to communicate with the Base Station during setup, you’ll be prompted by the app to press the Pairing button on the back of the Base Station until the blue ring begins spinning.



The first time your Base Station connects to the internet, it will automatically begin a software update, which may take a few minutes. The Base Station LED light should show a blue ring with yellow spinning around.



When it’s complete, the LED will be solid blue. Once your Base Station is set up, the LED light ring will use colour to tell you the status. Click here for an explanation of Base Station LED light patterns. Follow the app to continue the setup process.
